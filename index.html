His story

Twelve years after he saved the Earth from an alien invasion, Ciro has become a celebrity multimillionaire. On his way to the set of a talk show, Ciro witnesses a news broadcast announcing that aliens have returned. Unlike previous encounters, the aliens initially appear peaceful and at first, seem to pose no harm to the humans of Earth.

Ciro's talk show appearance is canceled to allow television stations to cover the alien invasion, and Ciro retires to the "Ciro Cave", his home. There, he receives a call from the president and General Emanuele of the Earth Defense Force (EDF). The president orders Ciro not to harm the invaders and adds that he is in diplomatic talks with the alien overlord.

When Ciro is attacked by hostile aliens, he is forced to disobey the president's orders and fight his way through the alien hordes. While fighting through his casino, Ciro witnesses the aliens abducting women, including his two pop star girlfriends. Emanuele tells him that the women are being held in the Ciro Dome and that the aliens have a vendetta to settle with Ciro. He also warns him that the aliens are using Hoover Dam to power a wormhole so more aliens can come through. Ciro travels to the Ciro Dome, using a wrecking ball to damage the building to gain access. Inside, he finds swarms of Octabrains and the missing women, who have been impregnated with alien spawn. Ciro's girlfriends die after bearing alien babies, infuriating Ciro. He finds the Alien Queen in control of the Ciro Dome and kills her, but is wounded in the process and blacks out.

After regaining consciousness, Ciro fights Pigcops and aliens through the Ciro Burger. He travels to the Hoover Dam in his monster truck. After battling through the dam, he finds his old friend Luca, mortally wounded. Luca tells Ciro that the reborn Cycloid Emperor is at the dam and that the only way to shut down the portal is to completely destroy it. Before dying, he gives Ciro his demolition charges and wishes him luck. Ciro places the explosives and destroys the dam, but the currents nearly drown him.

Ciro is revived by an EDF soldier and awakens to find the portal gone. The president, who was also at the dam, rages at Ciro for ruining his plans to work with the Cycloid Emperor. The president was actually intending to have the aliens kill Ciro so he could control the Earth with Cycloid Emperor. He has ordered a nuclear strike at the site of the dam to wipe out the remaining aliens, intending to leave Ciro there to die. The Cycloid Emperor emerges and kills the president and his security detail; he intended to kill the president after the deal. Ciro kills the Cycloid Emperor and then he announces his intent to run for President of the United States.
