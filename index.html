His story

One day after he saved the world from an alien invasion, Ciro has become a celebrity in nightclubs around the world. On his way to the set of a talk show, Ciro while he was drunk, witnesses a news broadcast announcing that aliens have returned ready to steal the beer of the earth. Unlike previous encounters, the aliens initially appear sober and not drunk at first, seem to pose no harm to the humans beer of Earth.

Ciro's talk show appearance is canceled, because while he was talking he had passed out for too much beer ingested, but they taken profit to allow television stations to cover the alien invasion, and Ciro retires to the "Ciro's Cave'", his home where there are almost only beers. There, he receives a call from the president and General "Pepp A Sguess" of the Earth Defense Force (EDF). The president orders Ciro not to harm the invaders and adds that he is in diplomatic talks with the alien overlord.

When Ciro is attacked by hostile aliens, he is forced to disobey the president's orders and fight his way through the alien hordes. Pepp A Sguess tells him that the women are being held in the Pleasure Hotel and that the aliens have a vendetta to settle with Ciro. He also warns him that the aliens are inseminating all the girls so more aliens can come through. Ciro travels to the Pleasure Hotel, using one of his wrecking ball to damage the building to gain access. Inside, he finds swarms of Octabrains and the missing women, who have been impregnated with alien spawn. Ciro's girlfriends die after bearing alien babies, infuriating Ciro. He finds the Alien Queen in control of the No money No Beers and kills her, but is drunk in the process and blacks out.

After regaining consciousness, Ciro get drunk again and fights aliens through the Ciro Burger. He travels to Napoli in his strunz truck. After battling through the Vulcano, he finds his old friend Luca, mortally wounded. Luca tells Ciro that the reborn Ommemmerd Emperor is at the Vulcano and that the only way to shut down the portal is to completely destroy it. Before dying, he gives Ciro his demolition charges and wishes him luck. Ciro places the explosives and destroys the Vulcano, but the lava nearly drown him.

Ciro is revived by an EDF soldier with a beer bottle and awakens to find the portal gone. The president, who was also at the dam, rages at Ciro for ruining his plans to work with the Ommemmerd Emperor. The president was actually intending to have the aliens kill Ciro so he could control the Earth. He has ordered a nuclear strike at the site of the Vulcano to wipe out the remaining aliens, intending to leave Ciro there to die. The Ommemmerd Emperor emerges and kills the president and his brainless security; he intended to kill the president after the deal. Ciro kills the Ommemmerd Emperor and then he announces his intent to run for President of Napoli.
